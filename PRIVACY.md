# ClipMenu Privacy Policy

_Last updated: 3 June 2026_

Host this page at a public URL (e.g. GitHub Pages) and enter that URL as the
**Privacy Policy URL** in App Store Connect. Replace `CONTACT_EMAIL` below with
the address you want users to reach you at. App Store Connect lets you set a
privacy-policy URL per localization, so all seven languages are provided.

---

## English

ClipMenu does not collect, transmit, sell, or share any personal data.

**What ClipMenu stores.** Your clipboard history and text snippets are stored
locally on your Mac. ClipMenu has no servers and sends no data to the developer
or to any third party.

**iCloud sync (optional).** If you turn on iCloud sync, your clipboard history
and snippets are synchronized through your own private iCloud account using
Apple's CloudKit. This content lives in your personal iCloud, is not accessible
to the developer, and is governed by Apple's Privacy Policy.

**Permissions.** Accessibility permission is used only to paste the item you
select into the frontmost app (by simulating Command-V). It is never used to
observe, log, or transmit your keystrokes or screen contents.

**No tracking.** ClipMenu contains no analytics, advertising, or third-party
tracking SDKs.

**Contact.** Questions: CONTACT_EMAIL.

**Changes.** Any updates to this policy will be posted on this page with a new
date above.
