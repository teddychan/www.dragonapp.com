Frequently asked questions

How do I open my clipboard history?
Press your global hotkey (set in Settings) to pop the history right at your cursor, or click the ClipMenu 2 icon in the menu bar.
ClipMenu 2 can't paste into other apps.
macOS requires Accessibility permission to paste for you. Open System Settings → Privacy & Security → Accessibility and enable ClipMenu 2.
What does the subscription include?
The Mac App Store edition is a paid app with a 30-day free trial, then an auto-renewing yearly subscription. It unlocks the full app and iCloud sync of your clipboard history and snippets across all your Macs.
How do I restore my subscription on a new Mac?
Open ClipMenu 2 and choose Restore Purchases on the welcome screen, signed in with the same Apple ID.
How do I manage or cancel the subscription?
Open the App Store app → click your name → Subscriptions, or visit your Apple subscriptions.
Where is my data stored?
Your history and snippets stay on your Mac and in your own private iCloud database. We never receive them. See our Privacy Policy.
